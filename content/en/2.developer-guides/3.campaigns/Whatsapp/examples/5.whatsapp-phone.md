This guide demonstrates how to orchestrate WhatsApp campaigns featuring **Call-to-Action (CTA)** phone buttons for immediate customer re-engagement.

::note
**Prerequisites**:
- Valid **Bearer Token**.
- **Digishare Message Template** (ITM or **WABA TM**) with an active call button component.
::

## 1. Operational Logic

::tip
Phone numbers are typically configured **statically** during the template governance phase in your Digishare Dashboard. This ensures architectural consistency across high-volume campaigns.
::

## 2. API Implementation

::api-playground
---
method: POST
url: https://api.digishare.ma/v1/campaigns
description: Send a campaign featuring an integrated phone call CTA.
variables:
  token: YOUR_TOKEN
  senderId: YOUR_SENDER_ID
  templateId: YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID
headers:
  Authorization: Bearer {token}
  Content-Type: application/json
body:
  title: Customer Support Outreach
  channel: whatsapp
  sender_label_id: "{senderId}"
  message_template_id: "{templateId}"
  recipients:
    - wa_id: "212600000000"
---
::
