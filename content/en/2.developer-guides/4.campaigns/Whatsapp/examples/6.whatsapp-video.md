This guide explains how to send a WhatsApp campaign including a **video**.

::note
**Prerequisites**:
- Valid **Bearer Token**.
- **Digishare Message Template** (ITM or **WABA TM**) configured to accept a video.
::

## 1. Payload Preparation

To send a video (MP4), provide the public URL of the video in the `header_url` field.

::api-playground{method="POST" url="https://api.digishare.ma/v1/campaigns" description="Send a personalized video." :variables='{"token": "YOUR_TOKEN", "senderId": "YOUR_SENDER_ID", "templateId": "YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID"}' :headers='{"Authorization": "Bearer {token}", "Content-Type": "application/json"}' :body='{"title": "Product Demo", "channel": "whatsapp", "sender_label_id": "{senderId}", "message_template_id": "{templateId}", "recipients": [{"wa_id": "212600000000", "header_url": "https://your-site.com/demo.mp4"}] }'}
::

::warning
The maximum video file size is **16 MB**. Ensure you use an URL that does not require authentication so that WhatsApp can download it.
::
