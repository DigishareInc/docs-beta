This guide explains how to send a WhatsApp campaign including a **Flow** (interactive journey).

::note
**Prerequisites**:
- Valid **Bearer Token**.
- **Digishare Message Template** (**WABA TM** type) configured with a Meta Flow.
- **Flow ID** and **Start Screen ID**.
::

## 1. Flow Functionality

Flows allow you to display forms and interactive screens directly within WhatsApp.

### Dynamic Parameters
You can pass initial data to the Flow via the `flow_token` and `flow_action_payload` objects.

## 2. Payload Example

::important
Flows are an advanced feature of **WABA TM** templates. Ensure your template is correctly approved by Meta before sending.
