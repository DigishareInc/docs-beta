This guide shows how to send a WhatsApp campaign including **Quick Reply** buttons.

::note
**Prerequisites**:
- Valid **Bearer Token**.
- **Digishare Message Template** (ITM or **WABA TM**) configured with Quick Reply buttons.
::

## 1. How it Works

Quick Reply buttons allow the user to respond with a single click. The value sent to your system upon clicking is the button text or its technical identifier.

## 2. Payload Structure

While button texts are defined within the template, you must trigger the campaign through the API.

::api-playground{method="POST" url="https://api.digishare.ma/v1/campaigns" description="Send Quick Reply buttons." :variables='{"token": "YOUR_TOKEN", "senderId": "YOUR_SENDER_ID", "templateId": "YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID"}' :headers='{"Authorization": "Bearer {token}", "Content-Type": "application/json"}' :body='{"title": "Survey Question", "channel": "whatsapp", "sender_label_id": "{senderId}", "message_template_id": "{templateId}", "recipients": [{"wa_id": "212600000000"}] }'}
::

::tip
Quick Reply buttons are ideal for surveys, appointment confirmations, or simple menu options.
::
