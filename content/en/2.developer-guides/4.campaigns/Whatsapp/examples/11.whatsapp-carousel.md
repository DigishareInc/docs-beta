This guide shows how to send a WhatsApp campaign including a **Carousel** of sliding cards (up to 10 cards).

::note
**Prerequisites**:
- Valid **Bearer Token**.
- **Digishare Message Template** (**WABA TM** type) configured for the Carousel format.
::

## 1. Carousel Card Structure

Each card in the carousel can have its own media (image/video) and text variables.

### Required Fields per Card
- `header_url`: Header image or video for the card.
- `body_variables`: Body variable values for the card.

## 2. Payload Example

::api-playground{method="POST" url="https://api.digishare.ma/v1/campaigns" description="Send a product Carousel." :variables='{"token": "YOUR_TOKEN", "senderId": "YOUR_SENDER_ID", "templateId": "YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID"}' :headers='{"Authorization": "Bearer {token}", "Content-Type": "application/json"}' :body='{"title": "Product Showcase", "channel": "whatsapp", "sender_label_id": "{senderId}", "message_template_id": "{templateId}", "recipients": [{"wa_id": "212600000000", "cards": [{"header_url": "https://site.com/p1.jpg", "body_variables": ["Vastux Pro", "199 USD"]}, {"header_url": "https://site.com/p2.jpg", "body_variables": ["Vastux Air", "99 USD"]}] }] }'}
::

::important
Carousels are exclusively available for Marketing-type **WABA TM** templates.
::
