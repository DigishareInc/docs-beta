## Key Benefits
- **Multi-channel integration**: Capture leads directly from Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok Ads, and custom landing pages.
- **Normalization engine**: Automatically maps and cleans field data (names, phone numbers, emails) regardless of the original format or language.
- **Zero data loss**: Unrecognized fields are stored as custom attributes, ensuring every piece of customer information is preserved.
