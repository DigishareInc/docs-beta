To maximize the efficiency of your lead capture process and ensure high-quality data in your CRM, follow these best practices.

## 1. Using Standardized Field Labels
While Digishare's normalization engine is powerful, we recommend using standard labels (like `full_name`, `email`, `phone_number`) wherever possible. This ensures the highest accuracy and reduces the need for manual review.

## 2. Phone Number Formatting
Encourage users to enter phone numbers in international format (e.g., `+212...`). Digishare will attempt to normalize local formats, but international ones are more reliable for global integrations.

## 3. Test Your Forms
Always use the testing tools provided by Meta and TikTok (like the Lead Ads Testing Tool) before launching your campaign. Verify that the leads appear correctly in your Digishare dashboard with all fields mapped as expected.

## 4. Leverage Custom Fields
Don't be afraid to ask platform-specific questions in your lead forms. Any field label not automatically mapped will be stored in the `custom_fields` attribute, providing you with all the context you need for a follow-up call.
