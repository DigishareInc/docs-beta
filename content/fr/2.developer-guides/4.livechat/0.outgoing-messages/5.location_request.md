---
title: Demande de Localisation
description: Demandez à l'utilisateur de partager sa localisation.
icon: i-mdi-send
---

Déclenche l'interface native "Envoyer la localisation".

::api-playground
---
method: POST
url: "https://api.digishare.ma/v1/messages"
headers:
  Authorization: "Bearer VOTRE_TOKEN"
  Content-Type: "application/json"
body:
  send_to_third: true
  conversation_id: "CONV_123"
  type: "interactive"
  body:
    type: "location_request_message"
    body:
      text: "Veuillez partager votre position actuelle."
    action:
      name: "send_location"
---
::
